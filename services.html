Examples of Problems We Can Solve

PROBLEM: I need a better understanding of my target customer.

OUR SOLUTION: We can synthesize market research, competitive and aspirational research, as well as trend reports specific to your target customer.


PROBLEM: You're looking to launch a new product line or brand and don’t have the headcount or capacity within your team.

OUR SOLUTION: We offer end-to-end design and product development from concept to production. We can make the process seamless and efficient.


PROBLEM: You’ve received customer feedback regarding quality or fit of your current production.

OUR SOLUTION: We’ll evaluate your current production against the desired spec as well as perform a competitive analysis with industry brand standards to offer solutions for fit consistency & quality improvements.


PROBLEM: You work with your factories directly on product development, but don’t have access to tech packs.

OUR SOLUTION: We will build custom Tech Packs to streamline and maintain brand information in one system. This will allow you to source with other factories/vendors to compare cost and quality.


PROBLEM: You’re investing in a new product lifecycle management software system, but don’t have the time or the headcount to transfer existing tech packs and libraries into the new system.

OUR SOLUTION: We have detail-oriented technicians that can not only enter your existing libraries and tech packs, but can streamline your information in the process.
